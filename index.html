About Me

Words as a way of seeing

I'm a writer based in New York, working at the intersection of culture, identity, and the stories we tell ourselves. My work has appeared in literary journals, brand publications, and digital magazines across three continents.

Before writing full-time, I spent years in editorial roles that taught me how good prose doesn't just inform — it transforms. I bring that same conviction to every piece I write.
